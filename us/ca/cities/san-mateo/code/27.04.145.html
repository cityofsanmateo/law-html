27.04.145 DENSITY.

"Density" means the intensity of use expressed as unit density.

(a) Unit density shall be based on the minimum lot area standards as designated in the zoning code.

(b) The density range descriptions for each district shall be as follows:

District Density Range
R1 Low
R2 Medium-low
R3 Medium
R4 High


(Ord. No. 1984-9 § 3; Ord. No. 1982-22 § 7; Ord. No. 1979-1 § 1.)
