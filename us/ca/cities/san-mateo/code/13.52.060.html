13.52.060 APPLICABILITY.

The provisions of this chapter shall apply to all persons or entities, including the City of San Mateo, or as specifically regulated under other provisions of the San Mateo Municipal Code. A person or entity who has received approval of a planning application which is also subject to the landscape regulations contained in Chapter 27.71, need only obtain a site development permit pursuant to Chapter 23.40 from the planning division for the removal and/or pruning of heritage trees on the property proposed for development.


(Ord. No. 2007-1 § 3; Ord. No. 2005-3 § 1; Ord. No. 2004-6 § 1; Ord. No. 1989-34 § 5; Ord. No. 1984-7 § 6; Ord. No. 1974-50 § 1; Ord. No. 1968-35 § 3; prior code § 65.55.)
