27.85.010 PURPOSE AND SCOPE.

The purpose of this chapter is to establish standards for day care centers. This chapter shall apply to day care centers, whether new or expanding, as "day care center" is defined in Section 27.04.130. This chapter shall not apply to "family day care home" as defined in Section 27.04.130.


(Ord. No. 1994-2 § 1.)
