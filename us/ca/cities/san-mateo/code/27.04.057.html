27.04.057 BEDROOM.

"Bedroom" means an enclosed habitable space in a structure which: (1) is designed primarily for sleeping; (2) meets the room dimension requirements of the most recent edition of the Uniform Building Code, as shall have been adopted by the City Council; and (3) contains one or more windows. A room having the potential of being a bedroom shall be considered a bedroom for density and parking calculation purposes.


(Ord. No. 1992-16 § 2; Ord. No. 1982-22 § 3; Ord. No. 1979-23 § 1.)
